About Me

I completed my Bachelors in Computer Engineering from Pune University in 2013. Then I pursued a professional career at the Bank of New York Mellon (iNautix Technologies) in India. I worked as a back-end developer in the team developing and maintaining modules for a number of projects, including constructing and maintaining online modules, stored procedures, batch jobs, formulating DB2 queries on IBM mainframes using COBOL, JCL, DB2, and MVS. I gained considerable experience at the company and became excellent at productive team dynamics and corporate behavior.

Challenges presented by the courses I took in my first year at UB helped me substantially extend my abilities to code and find solutions to difficult problems. I consistently worked hard to obtain the highest scores on all of the projects, as depicted on the leaderboard in Operating Systems where I achieved a perfect score in all of the programming assignments.

When I am not working or studying, I enjoy playing and watching tennis, hiking, reading books, traveling, trying new restaurants, attending colloquiums, and cooking.

Currently, I am actively searching for a full-time opportunity at a company having innovative projects and development initiatives in the Software/Security area. I am looking forward to apply the knowledge and experience that I gained during my Bachelors, Masters, and previous workplace.
