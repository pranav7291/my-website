About Me

For 22 months I worked at iNautix Technologies Private Limited, Pune, as part of the Entitlement Management Service for Pershing LLC’s revolutionary software NetX360. I worked as a back-end developer in the team and helped develop and maintain modules for a number of projects including constructing and maintaining online modules, stored procedures, batch jobs, formulating DB2 queries on IBM mainframes using COBOL, JCL, DB2, and MVS. I gained considerable professional experience at the company and am excellent at productive team dynamics and corporate behavior.

When I am not working or studying, I enjoy playing and watching tennis, hiking, reading books, traveling, trying new restaurants, attending colloquiums and plays, and cooking.

Coding marathons while working on the projects for the courses I took in my first year at UB - Information Retreival, Operating Systems, Machine Learning and Database Systems - helped me substantially push my limits to code and find solutions. I obtained perfect scores on most of these projects. A testimony to this is the leaderboard of the projects in Operating Systems where I achieved a perfect score in all of the programming assignments.

Currently, I am actively searching for a full-time opportunity at a Software/Security firm with a hope of gaining experience and mentorship from being a part of their innovative projects and development initiatives.
